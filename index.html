My software development journey

Introduction

My name is Kevin Bogunovic and I have decided to embark on a new and exciting journey learning software development with Correlation-One. The course will run for 21 weeks, throughout which I will gain valuable knowledge and skills in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and responsive design, Javascript, UI/UX design, front-end frameworks and CMS, amongst other things. This is my landing page, designed as part of my project work within the course, to tell you a little more about myself and showcase some of the things that I have learn't along the way.

About Me

I am 37 years old and live in Bedford, Bedfordhsire UK with my partner, 14 year old daughter, two sons aged 10 and 5 and my 2 cats. I currently work for Amazon as a L3 Logistics Coordinator in Cambridge LHR13 in the UK. I have worked for Amazon since August 2021 and have so far loved the experience. Having had nearly 10 years experience as an Operations Manager my experience and knowledge of logistics and customer service have certainly helped me succeed in this role to date. However, I have had an interest in this area for a long time and when the opportunity came up to particpate in this course I knew I had to take it! This is my first ever venture into software development, or any sort of coding, and there is a lot to take in. Although it's challenging at times, it's exciting learning the new concepts and I feel I am learning fast and gaining more confidence with every project I complete. I believe that my current skillset, coupled with the learnings from this course, are very suited to the ones needed to be a successful software developer and I am looking forward to transitioning into the software development field.
